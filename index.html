Mizoram’s First Local Taxi Aggregator

Welcome to Digiride — your trusted mobility partner designed for Mizoram’s unique roads and lifestyle. Whether you're commuting, shopping, or exploring, Digiride connects you with local drivers for safe and seamless rides. Key features include:

• Instant booking through the app
• QR or SMS-based pairing for offline access
• Real-time ride monitoring via Command Center
• SOS button for emergency support
• Transparent pricing with local driver empowerment

Digiride Technologies

Digiride is Mizoram’s first taxi aggregator, built for local roads and daily needs. Users can easily book rides through the app, with quick driver matching. For offline areas, the Easy Pair system enables QR or SMS-based taxi pairing. A live command center monitors every ride for safety and support. The SOS button allows instant emergency alerts to the support team. Digiride brings safe, smart, and reliable transport to the people of Mizoram.
